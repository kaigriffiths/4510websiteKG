2024 MAcc Course Rankings Student Exit Survey

This site summarizes course/program rankings computed from the 2024 graduate program exit survey. The analysis converts Likert responses to numeric scores (1–5), computes average ratings, ranks items, and generates a figure.

Outputs are produced by the Python script in src/analyze.py and saved in /output.
